About

I'm Tunde. A product manager, software developer and problem solver.

The cusp of technology and impact has always driven me. I've never shied away from exploring new solutions, whether it's diving into user research, market analysis, or rapid prototyping. From early days playing with LEGOs to understanding complex user flows, I've built a foundation for creating products that matter.

Fast forward to today, I've dabbled in various aspects of product development, from crafting user stories and competitive analyses to product roadmaps and agile methodologies. Every project, regardless of size, has been a stepping stone to refining my strategic thinking and empathy for users.

What ignites my passion? Turning insights into impactful products. It's more than just features and functionalities; it's about shaping experiences that truly solve problems, enhance lives, and drive business growth. Whether it's optimizing an e-commerce platform, streamlining internal workflows, or creating innovative solutions for underserved communities, understanding user needs and translating them into actionable plans is what excites me most.

This is one of my favourite quotes (by Marty Cagan):


"Winning products come from the deep understanding of the user’s needs combined with an equally deep understanding of what’s just now possible."


This resonates so deeply with me because I believe that the best products are born from the intersection of user needs and technological possibilities. It's not just about creating something new; it's about creating something that truly matters.

When I'm not crafting product strategies, you can find me writing music, playing the piano, or exploring new cuisines. I'm also a huge fan of video games and photography. I believe that creativity is a journey, and I'm always looking for new ways to express myself and connect with the world around me.
